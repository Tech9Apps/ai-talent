Job Description – Software Engineer
About the Role

We are looking for a Software Engineer to join our growing team. The ideal candidate has strong technical skills in C#/.NET, React, and cloud environments, and enjoys working on scalable applications that impact thousands of users daily.

Responsibilities

Design, develop, and maintain web applications using .NET and React/Next.js.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.

Write clean, testable, and maintainable code.

Participate in code reviews and contribute to continuous improvement.

Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize applications for performance and scalability.

Contribute to architectural discussions and propose innovative solutions.

Requirements

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience.

3+ years of experience with C#/.NET and modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Angular, or Vue).

Experience with REST APIs, SQL/NoSQL databases, and cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, or GCP).

Strong understanding of software design principles and CI/CD pipelines.

Good communication skills and ability to work in an agile team.

Nice to Have

Experience with Blazor or Firebase.

Knowledge of financial systems or fintech platforms.

Familiarity with DevOps practices and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).

Benefits

Competitive salary and performance bonuses.

Remote-first culture with flexible working hours.

Professional growth opportunities and budget for training/certifications.

Health insurance, paid time off, and wellness benefits.
